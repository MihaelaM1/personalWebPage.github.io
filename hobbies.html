My Hobbies

ANIME

Anime, short for "animation," is a distinct and influential form of storytelling that originated in Japan and has captivated audiences worldwide. Renowned for its unique art style, compelling narratives, and diverse range of genres, anime has become a global phenomenon, transcending cultural boundaries and captivating the hearts of millions.
One of her favourite anime is Jujutsu Kaisen, a critically acclaimed manga and anime series, has taken the world by storm with its gripping narrative, intense action, and intriguing supernatural elements. Created by Gege Akutami, Jujutsu Kaisen has captivated audiences with its dark and thrilling story set in a world where curses and sorcery collide.

BOOKS

Mihaela, a devoted fan of fantasy literature who finds solace and excitement in the realms of magic, mythical creatures, and epic adventures. Since a young age, Mihaela has been captivated by the enchanting worlds woven by fantasy authors, and their stories have become an integral part of their life.
From the moment Mihaela cracked open their first fantasy novel, they were transported to realms far beyond the boundaries of reality. They traversed the mystical landscapes of Tolkien's Middle-earth, soared on the wings of dragons in McCaffrey's Pern, and delved into the depths of magical schools like those in Rowling's Wizarding World. Each fantasy book brought a new universe to explore and ignited their imagination with wonder and awe.

DRAWING

Drawing is not just a hobby for Mihaela—it is an integral part of her identity and a means of self-expression. Through her art, she finds solace, joy, and a profound sense of fulfillment. Every stroke of her pencil or brush becomes a window into her soul, revealing her unique perspective on the world.
Mihaela's artistic journey is one of continuous exploration and growth. She embraces various mediums, from pencil and ink to pastels and watercolors, constantly pushing the boundaries of her artistic abilities. Her sketches, paintings, and illustrations reveal a versatile artist who fearlessly experiments with different styles and techniques.The act of drawing for Mihaela is a meditative process—a sanctuary where she can immerse herself in the present moment, lose track of time, and create something entirely her own.

MOVIES

For Mihaela, movies are more than just a form of entertainment. They are portals that transport her to different worlds, allowing her to experience a kaleidoscope of emotions, immerse herself in captivating narratives, and witness the power of visual storytelling. Whether it's a thrilling action flick, a thought-provoking drama, a heartwarming comedy, or a mesmerizing work of art-house cinema, each film leaves an indelible mark on her heart and mind.
Mihaela's love for movies goes beyond passive consumption. She actively engages with the medium, exploring the works of revered directors, analyzing film techniques, and delving into the rich tapestry of cinematic history. Some of her favourite movies are "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy, "Parasite", "Black Panther", "Avatar: The Way of Water" and many more.
